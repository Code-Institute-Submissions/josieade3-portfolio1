Cycle Circle

Why join cycle circle?

Fun

Cycling is a fun exercise activity that often reminds people of their childhood and removes the 'boring' stereotype about exercise

Health

Physical exercise, running included, is known to release endorphins in the brain.

Strength

Exercise can improve our immune system by increasing production of essential proteins and waking up lazy white blood cells.

Happiness

Physical exerciise makes your brain release endorphins which make you more energetic, more awake and happier. Who doesn't want to be happy?

What do we offer?

We offer cycling classes for ages 13-70, our classes are for everyone, we aim to make exercise fun while being inclusive!
