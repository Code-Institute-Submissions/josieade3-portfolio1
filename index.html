WHY JOIN CYCLE CIRCLE?

All of the benefits!

STRENGTH

Exercise can improve our immune system by increasing production of essential proteins and waking up lazy white blood cells.

HAPPINESS

Physical activity stimulates various brain chemicals that may leave you feeling happier, more relaxed and less anxious.

FUN

Cycling is a fun way to exercise, you get to enjoy the open air, sun on your face, and wind in your hair, plus the sights and sounds of the great outdoors.

HEALTH

Cycling strengthens your heart muscles, lowers resting pulse and reduces blood fat levels. Research also shows that people who cycle to work have two to three times less exposure to pollution than car commuters, so their lung function is improved.

SOCIAL

Enjoy the great outdoors and meet new friends while you exercise! Joining a cycling group can make you life-long friends and they can encourage you to exercise!

WELLNESS

Regular exercise may help ease depression and anxiety by: Releasing feel-good endorphins natural brain chemicals that can enhance your sense of well-being.
